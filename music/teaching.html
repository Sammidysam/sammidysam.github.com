--- title: Teaching --- Teaching

Contact Me I am interested in teaching if it interests you! The easiest way to get in touch is to email me at craigsamm@gmail.com.

Rates, etc. While I am still in college and in the midwest, I plan to charge $25 per lesson. This rate comes from my first teacher in Indiana charging $30 per lesson, and me not yet having my college degree but still putting a huge amount of time into music. However, I have not set my own rate for a while because I have been teaching through organizations. In any case, please do be in touch if this rate sounds like too steep of a hill to climb. I would never like financial constraints to limit musical growth.

Philosophy My biggest goal when teaching is for the material to be fun. If you love a song or a music group, let's explore that music and learn how it works and how we can play it! I happened to fall in love with classical music in the end, but it is not a requirement for you to. I am happy for us to chase your passion, together.

Past Experience I have been thrilled to get the opportunity to teach both in high school and in Oberlin. In high school, I started teaching bass during my junior year, and proceeded to have four students total over the next two years. At Oberlin, I have volunteered with the public school orchestra a fair amount. During my first year, I helped out Oberlin's high school orchestra bassists, then their middle school orchestra bassists during my second year. During my third year, I started teaching some bass students in the Northern Ohio Youth Orchestra privately. I also taught private lessons to some students in the Oberlin public school system through Oberlin Conservatory's String Pedagogy II class.
